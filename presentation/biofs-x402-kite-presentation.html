01 / THE CHALLENGE

Why AI Agents Need Payment Rails

! The Problem

  • GPU Costs: Variant calling costs $25-100 per genome - no micropayment solution exists
  • No Agent Identity: AI services can't autonomously negotiate, invoice, or receive payments
  • Data Silos: Genomic data locked in labs without patient consent tracking
  • Manual Billing: Each lab invoice requires human intervention and days of processing

✓ Our Solution

  • x402 Payments: Atomic micropayments in USDC on Avalanche - pay per variant call
  • Kite Passports: Each agent has DID, wallet, SLA guarantees, and reputation score
  • BioNFT Consent: Patient-controlled access via NFT-gated data on Sequentia
  • Auto-Settlement: Agents receive instant payment upon job completion
